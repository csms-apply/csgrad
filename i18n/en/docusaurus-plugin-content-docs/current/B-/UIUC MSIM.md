# UIUC MSIM

## Program Overview
The coursework is light. MSIM costs about $50K in tuition. It is suitable for job hunting and also has a co-op policy.

## Admission Preferences & Representative Data Points
Supports career changers to CS.
1. GIS undergrad, GPA 3.5, TOEFL 105 — admitted
2. 985 CS undergrad, GPA 3.6, TOEFL 109, two research experiences — admitted

## Job Outcomes & Data Points
Overall mediocre.

1. Indian student, CS undergrad from an unknown university, two years of full-time experience — landed at Amazon
2. NTNU undergrad, no internship — landed at a small company as a new grad
