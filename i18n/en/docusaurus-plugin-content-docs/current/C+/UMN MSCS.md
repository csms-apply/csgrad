# UMN MSCS

## Program Overview
A TA position can waive tuition and pays a monthly salary of about $1,600 after tax. UMN MSCS serves as a safety school for UMN BSCS students. Life is quite comfortable. If you are considering transferring to a PhD, this is worth looking into.

## Admission Preferences & Representative Data Points
C9 universities with high GPA (career changers) + 985/strong 211 universities with CS major and moderate GPA.

## Job Outcomes & Data Points
For big tech, it mainly relies on Amazon hiring. Overall, the job outcomes are not great.

## Transferring to PhD
If you find an advisor, you can transfer to a PhD program, which is a great deal. The UMN system is quite strong in research. If you want to go to another school, some students have received offers from strong programs such as Wisconsin, Purdue, and JHU. In most cases, students stay at UMN.
